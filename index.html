📌 What is the CSE?
The Civil Service Examination (CSE) is the Philippines' mandatory gateway to permanent government employment, administered by the Civil Service Commission (CSC). Passing it gives you civil service eligibility — the #1 legal requirement for any plantilla position.
⚠️ Over 80–85% of all test-takers fail every cycle. The national passing rate has hovered between 9–18% for the past decade. Structured, focused review is what separates passers from the rest.
